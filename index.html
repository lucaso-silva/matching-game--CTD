Rules

  • All the cards are mixed up and laid in cols and rows, face down on the table.
  • The player chooses, and turns over two cards:
  • ✖ If the cards do not match (it is not a pair), they are turned back over.
  • ✔ If the two cards match, it is a pair!
  • When the player found all the pairs, the game is over.

Difficult

  • Shih-Tzu: Easy mode, the player has to find 6 pairs.
  • Beagle: Medium mode, the player has to find 10 pairs.
  • Boxer: Hard mode, the player has to find 16 pairs.
