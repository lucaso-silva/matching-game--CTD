Matching Game

Difficult

Moves: 13

Time: 57s

Rules

  • All the cards are mixed up and laid in cols and rows, face down on the table.
  • Player choose, and turns over two cards:
  • If the cards do not match (it is not a pair), they turn them back over.
  • If the two cards match, it is a pair!
  • When the player found all the pairs, the game is over.

Difficult

  • Shih-Tzu: Easy mode, player has to find 6 pair.
  • Beagle: Medium mode, player have to find 10 pair.
  • Boxer: Hard mode, player have to find 16 pair.
